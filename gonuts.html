What was it about

Go nuts! is a game about a squirrel called Anton who needs to collect nuts to survive the winter. Luckily, he has a good chance to achieve it because many different sorts of nuts are falling from a tree.

First time with JS

It was also my very first contact with Javascript where I learnt the basics, like using event listeners or adding and removing classes to DOM elements. We also used setTimeout method to count down the time. The game was probably the most entertaining assignment during my studies and I would like to develop another, more advanced game one day. If you feel like, please give it a try
